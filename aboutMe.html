Most likely the first thing that others notice is that I am rather soft-spoken and introspective. Though it might seem arrogant, I like to believe that I am an honest if not blunt person given a natural setting. Otherwise, adjusting towards a given scenario or mood is not very difficult for me.

Even with these traits, I am still deeply competative. Even with the most mundane of tasks, I still strive to do my best or at least go beyond the scope of what others are doing. Call it what you will, competative flair or simply being too self-conscious, it is what it is.

I like to believe that I am also a fairly patient person. Though I like to banter and engage in that sort of talk, I am rarely quick to actually anger. I am also comfortable enought with myself where I can enjoy activites on my lonesome.

Personally, I believe that my work ethic is rather poor in terms of consistency over the course of a given track. Historically in both work and schooling, I prefer to finish off the bulk of my given work and duties as soon as they are assigned. Thus, over the course of the track, my contribution decreases day by day until I believe that I have acted sufficiently and have a product that satisfies my own criterion.

That being said, I also do not wish to deliver an unfinished project or assignment. Thus, historically, I have taken over other responsibilities that my coworkers or collegues should have taken. This has been the case both in terms of my schooling as well as my work.
